A General WvW guide for Scourge

Conditional Scourge.

Gear
We're going with Grieving and Trailblazers to get a mix of condi damage, power and sustain if you feel comfortable you can go full Grieving .

Runes & Sigils
For Runes: Good for boosting both damage types.

For Sigils: just trying to maximize both damage types.

Skill Bar

Healing Skill:Consume conditions is a very powerful condi clear,Sand flare can be used as well you'll gain some group support and a DPS increase from sadistic searing .

Utility Skills: Well of corruption is used on the big bomb, it does have a good cool down on it so make sure to use it wisely. Trail of anguish is are stun break.Dessicate is a good life force generator

Elite Skills: Ghastly breach is a very powerful skill if you can trap the enemy in it you're almost certain to get downs.Try using it inside a Warriors winds or on a big group of downs .

Specs

Use the build shown above unless you're having problems or feel comfortable enough to test something new.

Curses: We will be taking curses for the extra broom corrupt from path of corruption and the sustain from parasitic contagion
Soul Reaping: We will be taking Soul Reaping for the extra life force generation and the extra damage from dhuumfire.
Scourge: Nourishing Rot is good in small-scale but if life force generation isn't an issue use fell beacon.

General Play

KiS plays Scourge very aggressive. All your shade skills triggers where you place the shade and also on you, turning you into a walking AOE this gives you the ability to hit 10 targets, you want to take full advantage of this by placing yourself on the enemy and your shade where you think the enemy will be.

Shade's
This will be your main source of damage be mindful of the charge count, but make sure to have one up at all times you gain 225 extra expertise(20%Condition duration) and 15% damage reduction as long as you have an active shade. Try to place it where you think a large group of the enemy will be, they have a 0.5 sec delay and a very clear tell which makes it easy to avoid, if you see a Warriors bubble drop one in it immediately. Whenever you trigger any shade ability it will also pulse from you making Scourge a huge threat in melee distance(300 radius).Activating any shade skill will add a stack of burning with 30-40% of our damage coming from burning this is a big part of it

A low CD boon corrupt good for applying pressure and some added damage. If life force isn't an issue keep this on cool down
